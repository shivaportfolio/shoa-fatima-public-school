About Our School

A Legacy of Learning & Excellence

Shoa Fatima Public School has been a beacon of quality education in Lucknow since 2005. We are affiliated with CBSE and offer a holistic curriculum from Nursery to Class XII.

Our philosophy blends academic rigor with values, arts, sports, and technology — preparing students not just for examinations but for life.

📖

Academic Excellence

Structured curriculum with expert faculty, regular assessments and personalized attention for every learner.

🌱

Character Building

Inculcating values of honesty, empathy, and responsibility through daily practice and community events.

🚀

Innovation & Technology

Smart classrooms, coding labs, and STEM programs that prepare students for the future economy.
